ABOUT

MillicentMaker--but you can call me Millie. I'm a graphic designer out of Coeur d'Alene, Idaho, and I cannot think of a better place for my business to grow. It all started here, and then I moved to Cheney, Washington for a few years to attend Eastern Washington University where I received by Bachelors of Visual Communication Design. I love to work with like minded, creative, entrepreneurs who are just as picky about design as I am. I am currently accepting clients, so if you have an idea for a project, I would love to hear from you!
